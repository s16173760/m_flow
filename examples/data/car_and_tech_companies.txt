M-flow processes diverse data sources to build knowledge graphs. This sample
text covers topics in automotive engineering and technology manufacturing.

Electric vehicles have transformed the automotive industry. Companies like
Tesla, Rivian, and Lucid Motors are leading the transition from internal
combustion engines to battery-electric powertrains. Key challenges include
battery energy density, charging infrastructure, and manufacturing costs.

In the technology sector, semiconductor companies play a crucial role.
TSMC, Samsung, and Intel manufacture the chips that power everything from
smartphones to data centers. The shift to 3nm and 2nm process nodes
continues to push the boundaries of Moore's Law.

Autonomous driving technology combines computer vision, lidar sensing,
and machine learning to enable vehicles to navigate without human input.
Companies invest billions in developing Level 4 and Level 5 autonomous
systems, with safety validation requiring millions of miles of testing.
